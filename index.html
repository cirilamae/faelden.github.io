Dual Personality

What is it like in you, by Cirila Mae

Do you find yourself really shy and reserved around new people but really loud around people you know and people you love? And do you find it difficult to classify yourself between introvert and extrovert? Then the millennial era has found a new word to explain your personality that is ambivert. Ambivert people are the mix of personality factors taken from both shy and loud people and the personality which is unleashed at a given point of time depends on their surrounding and the people, pretty scientific process I would say. And we have brought the best Ambivert Quotes and Sayings for these people. Being an ambivert might feel like having split personalities. Sometimes you feel like you are having the best time among people and you dance and you talk and you are on top of the world. But then life throws some new people around you and you feel like killing yourself because of the discomfort and anxiety.
